# Helios Billing Policy

**Effective Date:** January 1, 2024
**Last Updated:** February 20, 2025
**Policy ID:** POL-BILLING-001

## Billing Cycles

Helios supports two billing cycles: **monthly** and **annual**. Monthly plans are billed on the same calendar day each month. Annual plans are billed once per year on the anniversary of the subscription start date. Customers may switch between monthly and annual billing at any time through their account settings.

## Payment Methods

Helios accepts major credit cards (Visa, Mastercard, American Express) and select digital payment methods. Customers are responsible for keeping their payment information current. Payment details can be updated in the Account Settings section of the workspace.

## Failed Payments

When a scheduled payment fails, Helios initiates the following retry sequence:

1. **First retry:** 24 hours after the initial failure.
2. **Second retry:** 3 days after the initial failure.
3. **Third retry:** 7 days after the initial failure.

The customer receives an email notification after each failed attempt. If all three retry attempts fail, the account is **automatically downgraded to the Free plan**. Workspace data is retained for 30 days after downgrade, during which the customer can reactivate their paid plan by updating their payment method.

## Annual Subscription Auto-Renewal

Annual subscriptions automatically renew at the end of each billing year unless the customer cancels at least **30 days before the renewal date**. A renewal reminder email is sent 45 days and 15 days before the renewal date. Customers who miss the 30-day cancellation window will be charged for the next annual term and must request a refund under the Refund Policy.

## Plan Upgrades and Downgrades

Plan changes take effect **immediately** upon confirmation. When upgrading, the customer is charged a pro-rata amount for the remainder of the current billing period at the new plan rate. When downgrading, a pro-rata credit is applied to the next billing cycle. No cash refunds are issued for downgrades; credits are applied automatically.

## Billing Disputes

Customers who believe they were charged incorrectly should contact support within 60 days of the charge. The support agent should look up the customer's billing history and subscription status before responding. If a duplicate charge is confirmed, escalate to the Billing team for correction.

## Agent Instructions

- Always look up the customer's subscription and billing status before addressing billing questions.
- Do not make billing adjustments directly; escalate confirmed issues to the Billing team.
- Cite this policy when explaining billing rules to customers.

# AcmeFlow Refund Policy

**Effective Date:** January 1, 2024
**Last Updated:** March 15, 2025
**Policy ID:** POL-REFUND-001

## Eligibility Window

AcmeFlow offers a full refund within **30 calendar days** of the original purchase date for all paid plans (Pro, Business, and Enterprise). The 30-day window is calculated from the date of the initial charge or the most recent renewal charge, whichever is later.

## Pro-Rata Refunds for Annual Plans

Customers on annual billing cycles who cancel within the 30-day window of their most recent renewal are entitled to a pro-rata refund. The refund amount is calculated as the unused portion of the annual term, minus any discounts applied at the time of purchase. Monthly plan customers who cancel within the 30-day window receive a full refund of their most recent monthly charge.

## After the 30-Day Window

No refunds are issued after the 30-day eligibility window has passed. This applies to all plan types. Customers who cancel after 30 days retain access to their paid features until the end of the current billing period, at which point the account reverts to the Free plan.

## Enterprise Plan Exceptions

Enterprise plan refund requests are handled exclusively by the AcmeFlow Account Management team. Support agents should not process or promise Enterprise refunds directly. Instead, create an escalation to the Account Management team with the customer's details and refund request. Enterprise customers with custom contracts may have separate refund terms outlined in their service agreement.

## Refund Processing

Approved refunds are processed to the original payment method within **5 to 10 business days**. AcmeFlow does not issue refunds via alternative payment methods, store credits, or checks. If the original payment method is no longer valid, the customer should contact their financial institution after the refund is initiated.

## Free Plan

The Free plan incurs no charges. No refund is applicable for Free plan accounts. If a Free plan customer believes they were charged in error, escalate the issue to the Billing team for investigation.

## Agent Instructions

- Always verify refund eligibility using the `check_refund_eligibility` tool before making any commitment.
- Never promise a refund without confirming eligibility.
- Cite this policy when explaining refund decisions to customers.
- Escalate Enterprise refund requests to Account Management.

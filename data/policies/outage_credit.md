# AcmeFlow Outage Credit Policy

**Effective Date:** January 1, 2024
**Last Updated:** February 5, 2025
**Policy ID:** POL-OUTAGE-001

## Service Level Agreement (SLA)

AcmeFlow provides the following uptime guarantees based on plan tier:

| Plan | Uptime SLA |
|------|-----------|
| Free | No SLA (best effort) |
| Pro | 99.9% monthly uptime |
| Business | 99.9% monthly uptime |
| Enterprise | 99.95% monthly uptime |

Uptime is calculated as the percentage of total minutes in a calendar month during which the AcmeFlow platform is available and functional. Scheduled maintenance windows, announced at least 48 hours in advance, are excluded from uptime calculations.

## Credit Calculation

When the platform fails to meet the SLA for a customer's plan tier, service credits are issued as follows:

- **Credit rate:** 10% of the customer's monthly bill for each full hour of downtime exceeding the SLA threshold.
- **Maximum credit:** 100% of the customer's monthly bill for the affected period.
- **Minimum downtime for credit:** The cumulative downtime must exceed the SLA threshold before credits are applicable. For a 99.9% SLA, this means more than approximately 43 minutes of unscheduled downtime in a calendar month.

Credits are calculated based on the customer's current monthly rate, regardless of billing cycle. Annual plan customers receive credits based on their effective monthly amount (annual price divided by 12).

## Credit Application

Approved credits are applied **automatically to the next billing cycle**. Credits cannot be converted to cash, transferred to another account, or applied retroactively to previous billing periods. If a customer's account is canceled before the credit is applied, the credit is forfeited.

## Request Window

Customers must submit outage credit requests within **30 days** of the incident date. Requests submitted after the 30-day window are not eligible for credits. The agent should document the incident date and verify it falls within the request window before processing.

## Free Plan Limitations

Free plan accounts do not have an SLA and are not eligible for outage credits. If a Free plan customer requests an outage credit, the agent should explain the SLA tiers and suggest upgrading to a paid plan for guaranteed uptime.

## Enterprise Exceptions

Enterprise customers with custom SLA agreements may have different credit terms as specified in their service contract. Enterprise outage credit requests should be escalated to the Account Management team, who will review the customer's specific contractual terms.

## Agent Instructions

- Verify the customer's plan tier and SLA before processing credit requests.
- Confirm the incident date falls within the 30-day request window.
- Calculate credits based on the documented formula.
- Escalate Enterprise credit requests to Account Management.
- Cite this policy when explaining credit eligibility to customers.
